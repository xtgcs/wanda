Our Businesses

Wanda Sports is owner and operator of key events, strategic partner to sports rights holders, and frontrunner in digital innovation

· Spectator Sports

We cover Football, Winter Sports and Summer Sports where we provide sports marketing services and solutions to sports rights holders.

· Digital, Production, Sports Solutions (DPSS)

We have developed a strong digital solutions platform to maximize the potential of our sports events and marketing business across production, innovative content development and advertising etc.

· Mass Participation

We own, organize, operate and license mass participation sports events, including triathlon, running, mountain biking, road cycling and obstacle course racing.
